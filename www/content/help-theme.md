## Theme

Sets the overall visual style of the plot using ggplot2's built-in themes.

- **Minimal** — light gray axis lines and gridlines, no border. Clean and modern; good default for most uses.
- **Classic** — axis lines only, no gridlines, white background. Resembles base R and traditional publication figures.
- **Black & white** — black-and-white with a full panel border and gridlines. Useful when color is already carrying a lot of information.
- **Void** — no axes, no gridlines, no background. For purely visual presentations or when axes are not informative.
