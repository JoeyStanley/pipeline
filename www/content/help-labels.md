## Labels

Add a title, subtitle, and custom axis labels to the plot. These are passed directly to ggplot2 and will appear in both the on-screen display and any downloaded figure.

Leave fields blank to omit that element entirely. The default axis labels **F2** and **F1** follow the convention of plotting F2 on the x-axis and F1 on the y-axis (both with high values toward the origin, matching the traditional vowel quadrilateral orientation).
