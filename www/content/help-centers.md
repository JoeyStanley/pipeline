## Centers

Placeholder. Centers mark the central tendency of each vowel class — a single point representing where the vowel "lives" in the F1/F2 space.

### Means vs. medians

- **Means** — the arithmetic average of F1 and F2 across all tokens. Sensitive to outliers.
- **Medians** — the middle value. More robust when outliers are present.

### 95% CI crosshairs

Available when **Means** is selected. Draws horizontal and vertical error bars representing the 95% confidence interval around the mean F1 and mean F2. Wider crosshairs indicate more variability or fewer tokens.

### One label per…

Controls the grouping level for center markers. **Phoneme** shows one marker per Wells keyword (e.g., FLEECE); **allophone** shows one per phonological environment (e.g., FLEECE-elsewhere, FLEECE-prelateral).
